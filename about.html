Our Story

"We started VANTA because we were tired of consulting firms that sold decks instead of delivering results."

— Reza Mahardika, Founder & CEO

VANTA was founded in 2018 by a small team of engineers and strategists who had worked at some of the largest tech companies in Southeast Asia. We saw the same pattern repeat: enterprises paid for transformation projects that shipped late, over budget, and underdelivered. We built VANTA to change that.

We operate on a single principle — honesty before optics. We tell clients what they need to hear, not what keeps them comfortable. That means walking away from projects that aren't the right fit, and doubling down when we know we can make a difference.

Today we are a 60-person firm headquartered in Jakarta, with satellite teams in Singapore and Surabaya. We work with mid-market companies and large enterprises across fintech, logistics, e-commerce, and government services.

Our work spans the full lifecycle — from early-stage discovery and architecture to post-launch support and team upskilling. We don't disappear after delivery; we stick around until the system actually works in the real world.
